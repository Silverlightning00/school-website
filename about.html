About Pokéschool

General Information

The school is an alternative option to Pokémon Trainers who don't want to go around to collect Gym Badges. Once a student graduates from Pokéschool, they automatically qualify for the Pokémon League.

Pokéschool is located in Saffron City in the Kanto region.

Our school colors are red, blue, yellow, and white.

Our school mascot is Pikachu.

School Hours are 7:20AM - 2:10PM with a seven period day.

Our Mission

The mission of Pokéschool is to provide each student with the opportunity to become the best Pokémon trainer they can be by adapting to their battle style, encouraging them to learn new things, and challenging students to think critically to overcome any obstacle they may face.

Map and Location

We are located in one of the busiest cities in Kanto, Saffron City. Pokeschool can be found in the far west side of Saffron City.

There are four routes that connect to Saffron City. These routes are route 5 to the north, route 6 to the south, route 7 to the west, and route 8 to the east.
