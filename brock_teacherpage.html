Brock

Rock Type Pokemon Teacher

Brock is from Pewter City in the Kanto Region. He is quite proud of his rock-hard willpower, and uses his Rock-type Pokémon to complement this. Learn everything there is to know about rock type Pokemon, moves, and abilities. In this class, you will learn how to battlle and train your rock type Pokemon! The final exam will also include (like every other type specific teacher) a battle with the teacher.
