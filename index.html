News and Announcements

Spirit Week

It’s spirit week! Spirit week lasts from the 13th to the 17th. Dress as your favorite Pokémon of all time and fill the school with Pokémon all week long. The days of the week and their respective themes are listed below.

  • Monday: Starters. Dress as any of the starter Pokémon from any region.
  • Tuesday: Pikachu. Dress as our school mascot, Pikachu!
  • Wednesday: Old school. Dress as one of the first 150 Pokémon discovered.
  • Thursday: Alola. Dress as one of the Pokémon from the Alola region.
  • Friday: Free Choice. Pick any Pokémon you like to dress as. Pick a Pokémon you dressed as before or pick something totally new!

Buses and Transportation

Buses will be able to pick up anyone in the following cities and towns in the Kanto region:

  • Cerulean City
  • Vermilion City
  • Lavender Town
  • Celadon City
  • Saffron City

The magnet train located in Saffron City can be used to get from Goldenrod City in the Johto Region to Pokeschool if you wish to attend this school from the Johto Region.
